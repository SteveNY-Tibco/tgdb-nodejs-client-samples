Stem Cell Database:
Discovering Value from Relationships

New discoveries can be found through analyzing relationships. TIBCO Graph DB has the ability to draw relationships out for users to discover correlations between data points or groups.

In this demo, we have gathered stem cell protein IDs and correlation indexes. The correlation indexes shows the probability of one gene ID affecting another.

With this visualization, we can extract more value from the dataset by discovering distant relationships.
